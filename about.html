Our Story

Leading the Way in Logistics

IGR Courier & Cargo was founded with a simple mission: to make logistics seamless for everyone. We are a reliable logistics and cargo service provider offering efficient, secure, and cost-effective delivery solutions.

Specializing in courier, parcel, and cargo transportation, the company serves individuals and businesses with timely pickups, safe handling, and dependable delivery. With a strong operational network and customer-focused approach, IGR Courier & Cargo ensures smooth logistics operations and consistent service quality across destinations.
